ACE Research Group

Advancing Distributed Real-Time and Embedded Systems

The ADAPTIVE Communication Environment (ACE) research group focuses on developing innovative middleware frameworks, patterns, and tools that facilitate the development of distributed real-time and embedded (DRE) systems.
